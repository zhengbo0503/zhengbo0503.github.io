I am a fourth Ph.D. student in the [Department of Mathematics](https://www.maths.manchester.ac.uk/) at the [University of Manchester](https://www.manchester.ac.uk/). I amsupervised by [Dr. Marcus Webb](https://personalpages.manchester.ac.uk/staff/marcus.webb/) and [Prof. Françoise Tisseur](https://personal.maths.manchester.ac.uk/tisseur/). My research interest lies on numerical linear algebra, matrix analysis and high performance computing. My particular focus is on developing mixed-precision Jacobi algorithms for computing accurate eigensystems of symmetric positive definite matrix and singular value system for general matrix.
